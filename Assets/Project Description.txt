Project Description:
We are setting out to make a space shooter, in the vein of asteroids

Learning Objectives:
	Intro to file mgmt
	Intro to game scripting
	Intro to GUI elements
	Intro to Input
	Intro to Events
	Intro to Scene Loading

Production Time:
	Setup 10 minutes
	Assets 45 minutes
	Player 30 minutes
	Enemy 20 minutes
	Menus 40 minutes
	GUI 30 minutes
	Build 10 minutes
	Review 10 minutes

High Concept:
	A space shooter where the player must avoid asteroids that try to destrou their ship. Score as many points as you can by shooting the asteroids in a given amount of time.

Controls:
	Arrow keys to move ship left, right, up, down
	Space bar to shoot
	C create a temporary shield
